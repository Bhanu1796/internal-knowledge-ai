# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Overview

The company reimburses employees for reasonable, pre-approved business expenses incurred in the course of their work. This policy outlines eligible expenses, approval requirements, and how to submit a claim.

## Eligible Expenses

### Travel
- Economy-class flights for business travel (business class requires VP-level approval)
- Train or bus tickets
- Taxi or rideshare to/from airport or client site
- Personal vehicle mileage at the current approved rate: **₹ 12 per km**

### Accommodation
- Hotel stays for business travel up to **₹ 8,000 per night** (metro cities)
- Hotel stays up to **₹ 5,000 per night** (non-metro)
- Exceeding limits requires prior written approval from your department head

### Meals
- Breakfast: up to ₹ 300
- Lunch: up to ₹ 500
- Dinner: up to ₹ 700
- Client entertainment meals: up to ₹ 2,000 per person with prior approval

### Other
- Conference registration fees (pre-approval required)
- Business stationery and printing
- Internet/data charges while travelling on business (up to ₹ 500/day)

## Ineligible Expenses

- Personal meals when not travelling
- Alcohol (unless part of a pre-approved client entertainment event)
- Personal entertainment (movies, gym, etc.)
- Traffic fines and parking violations
- Spouse or family member travel costs

## How to Submit a Claim

1. Collect original receipts for all expenses
2. Log in to the **HR Portal** at **hr-portal.internal.com**
3. Navigate to **Finance → Expense Claim**
4. Enter each expense item with date, category, amount, and description
5. Upload photos of receipts (PDF or JPEG)
6. Submit for manager approval

Claims must be submitted within **30 days** of incurring the expense. Claims older than 30 days will not be processed without a written exception from Finance.

## Approval and Payment

- Manager approves the claim in the HR Portal
- Finance reviews and processes within 5 business days of approval
- Reimbursement is paid with the next monthly payroll cycle or via direct transfer if marked urgent

## Advances

Travel advances up to ₹ 20,000 can be requested via Finance for upcoming travel. Contact **payroll@company.internal** at least 5 business days before travel.

## Contact

For queries, contact the Finance team at **payroll@company.internal**.

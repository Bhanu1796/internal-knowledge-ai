# Remote Work Policy

## Overview

The company supports flexible working arrangements. This policy sets out who is eligible to work remotely, the requirements employees must meet, and the responsibilities of both employees and managers.

## Eligibility

Remote work is available to employees who:

- Have completed their probation period (minimum 3 months)
- Have a role that can be performed effectively without physical presence
- Have demonstrated consistent performance and reliability
- Have a suitable home working environment (see requirements below)

Roles that require on-site presence (e.g. facilities, reception, lab work) are not eligible for remote work.

## Work Arrangement Options

| Arrangement | Description |
|---|---|
| Hybrid (default) | Minimum 3 days in office per week; remaining days remote |
| Fully Remote | Available only with VP-level approval and for specific roles |
| Temporary Remote | Short-term (up to 4 weeks) for personal circumstances — requires manager approval |

The default company arrangement is **hybrid**. Department heads may set stricter in-office requirements depending on team needs.

## Home Office Requirements

Employees working remotely must ensure:

- A dedicated, quiet workspace free from interruptions during working hours
- A stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download)
- Company-issued laptop used for all work (personal devices not permitted for accessing company systems)
- VPN connected at all times when accessing internal systems
- Webcam available for video calls (mandatory for team meetings)

## Working Hours

Remote employees must be available and responsive during core hours: **10:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST** on working days. Outside core hours, flexible scheduling is permitted with manager agreement.

## Security Requirements

- Lock your screen when stepping away from your workstation
- Do not work from public Wi-Fi without VPN active
- Do not allow family members or guests to use your company device
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately to IT at **it-helpdesk@company.internal**

Refer to the **IT Security Guidelines** for the full list of security requirements.

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop. Employees are responsible for their own desk, chair, and internet connection. A one-time home office allowance of ₹ 10,000 is available for new remote-eligible employees — apply via the HR Portal.

## Manager Responsibilities

Managers must:
- Ensure remote employees are included in team meetings and communications
- Schedule at least one video check-in per week per direct report
- Monitor performance and raise concerns promptly

## Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually. The current version is effective from January 2026.

## Contact

For queries, contact HR at **hr@company.internal**.

# Performance Review Process

## Overview

The company conducts formal performance reviews twice a year to recognise achievement, identify development opportunities, and align individual goals with company objectives.

## Review Cycle

| Review | Period Covered | Completion Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Mid-Year Review | January – June | 31 July |
| Annual Review | January – December | 31 January (following year) |

New employees who join after 1 October will have their first formal review at the Mid-Year Review of the following year. An informal 90-day check-in is conducted by the line manager instead.

## Rating Scale

Performance is assessed on a 5-point scale:

| Rating | Label | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | Outstanding | Consistently exceeds all expectations; significant impact beyond role |
| 4 | Exceeds Expectations | Regularly surpasses role requirements |
| 3 | Meets Expectations | Fully delivers on all role responsibilities |
| 2 | Needs Improvement | Partially meets expectations; improvement plan required |
| 1 | Unsatisfactory | Fails to meet core requirements; formal action required |

## Review Process — Step by Step

### Step 1: Self-Assessment (Employee)
- Log in to the HR Portal at **hr-portal.internal.com**
- Navigate to **Performance → Self Assessment**
- Complete the self-assessment form covering goals, achievements, and development areas
- Submit at least 5 business days before your scheduled review meeting

### Step 2: Manager Assessment
- Your line manager reviews your self-assessment
- The manager completes their rating and written comments for each competency
- Manager assessment is submitted before the review meeting

### Step 3: Review Meeting
- A 1:1 meeting between employee and manager (30–60 minutes)
- Discussion of ratings, feedback, and development plan
- Goals for the next review period are agreed upon

### Step 4: Calibration (Manager and Above)
- Department heads calibrate ratings across teams to ensure consistency
- Calibration does not change individual feedback, only normalises ratings distribution

### Step 5: Final Rating and Sign-Off
- Employee receives the final review summary via HR Portal
- Employee must acknowledge (sign off) within 5 business days
- Employees may add written comments if they disagree with the rating

## Competencies Assessed

All employees are assessed on:
- Quality of work and technical skills
- Communication and collaboration
- Problem-solving and initiative
- Time management and reliability
- Alignment with company values

## Link to Compensation

Annual salary reviews are conducted alongside the Annual Review. Compensation changes are effective from 1 April and are communicated separately by HR after calibration is complete.

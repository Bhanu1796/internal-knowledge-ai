# Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome

Welcome to the company! This guide covers everything you need to get set up in your first week. If you have any questions not covered here, reach out to your assigned onboarding buddy or the HR team at **hr@company.internal**.

## Day 1 Checklist

- [ ] Collect your access badge from Reception (Ground Floor, Building A)
- [ ] Meet your line manager and receive your laptop
- [ ] Sign your employment contract and tax forms with HR
- [ ] Set up your company email (your IT contact will assist)
- [ ] Join the company-wide Slack workspace — invite link sent to personal email
- [ ] Complete mandatory security awareness training (link in welcome email)

## IT Setup

Your IT contact for onboarding is listed in your welcome email. On Day 1, IT will provision:

- Company laptop (Windows 11 or MacOS depending on role)
- Microsoft 365 account (email, Teams, SharePoint access)
- VPN access (Cisco AnyConnect)
- Standard software bundle (see IT Software Request Policy)

For any additional software, refer to the **IT Software Request Policy** and raise a ticket via the IT Portal at **it-portal.internal.com**.

### Setting Up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA is mandatory for all company accounts.

1. Download the **Microsoft Authenticator** app on your mobile device
2. Go to **aka.ms/mfasetup** and sign in with your company credentials
3. Follow the prompts to register your device
4. Contact IT Helpdesk at extension **4000** if you encounter issues

## HR Setup

Complete the following via the **HR Portal** at **hr-portal.internal.com**:

- Personal details and emergency contact
- Bank account details for payroll
- Tax declaration form
- Benefits enrolment (health insurance, pension)

Payroll cutoff is the 25th of each month. If you join after the 25th, your first paycheck will include salary for both the partial joining month and the following month.

## First Week Schedule

- **Day 1**: IT setup, HR paperwork, office tour
- **Day 2**: Team introductions, tool walkthroughs with your manager
- **Day 3–4**: Role-specific onboarding sessions (scheduled by your manager)
- **Day 5**: Meet-and-greet with department heads (optional but recommended)

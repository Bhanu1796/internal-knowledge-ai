# Annual Leave Policy

## Overview

All permanent employees are entitled to paid annual leave. This policy outlines entitlement, accrual, how to apply, and rules around carry-over and encashment.

## Leave Entitlement

- **0–2 years of service**: 20 working days per calendar year
- **2–5 years of service**: 25 working days per calendar year
- **5+ years of service**: 30 working days per calendar year

Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis calculated against their contracted hours.

## Accrual

Leave accrues monthly at the rate of 1/12 of the annual entitlement. Accrual begins from the employee's confirmed start date. During the probation period, leave accrues but may not be taken until probation is successfully completed.

## How to Apply for Annual Leave

1. Log in to the HR Portal at **hr-portal.internal.com**
2. Navigate to **My Leave → Apply for Leave**
3. Select leave type: **Annual Leave**
4. Choose start and end dates
5. Add an optional note for your manager
6. Submit the request

Your direct manager will receive an email notification and must approve or reject within 3 business days.

## Notice Period

Employees must give a minimum notice of:
- 1 day for leave of 1–2 days
- 5 business days for leave of 3–9 days
- 10 business days for leave of 10 or more consecutive days

## Carry-Over

Up to 10 unused leave days may be carried over to the following calendar year. Carried-over leave must be used by 31 March of the following year or it will lapse. Exceptions require written approval from the HR Director.

## Leave Encashment

Encashment of unused annual leave is not permitted except upon resignation or termination, in which case accrued and unused leave will be paid out at the employee's daily basic salary rate.

## Public Holidays

Public holidays are not counted as annual leave days. If a public holiday falls during a period of approved annual leave, the employee will be credited one additional leave day.

## Contact

For queries, contact the HR team at **hr@company.internal** or raise a ticket via the HR Portal.
